###### Installation
1. Download the archive, unzip it
2. Copy the contents from the unzipped folder to files into phpBB/ext/showandclosebox
3. Navigate into the ACP to Customise -> Manage extensions
4. Go to "ACP" > "Customise" > "Extensions" and enable the "showandclosebox" extension
5. Clear all caches

####### Styling
Create your IF-Statement(s), text, links for the boxes ecetera into the language folders. Also set your links for three small images (look at the examples). So take a look into the event (HTML)
ext/showandclosebox/showandcloseyourbox/styles/StarTrekExcerpts/template/event/overall_footer_after.html for some instructions, links.

Languages: showandcloseyourbox.php
Please fill the "placeholder" ("#") with your link you want to use FOR INFORMATIONS ( line 47 ).

Clear all caches, also browser-caches after your works and please test your results.
